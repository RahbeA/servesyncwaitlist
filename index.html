Volunteering Made


ServeSync simplifies the process of finding meaningful volunteer work. Whether you're looking to enhance your resume, gain experience, or make a difference in your community, we help you connect with causes that matter to you. Browse opportunities, sign up, and start volunteering in just a few clicks.


Create An Account.


Enter in your personal information to set up you account so you can serve.


Customize Your Profile.


Enter in any other extra details about yourself you'd like organizations to know.


Find Opportunities Near You.


Search through our wide variety of opportunities using filters to make it as convineint as possible.


Start Serving.


Go out in your community and become a contributing member to help work towards something great!

Frequently Asked Questions

Addressing Common Inquiries

How does ServeSync work?


ServeSync allows students to easily find and sign up for volunteering opportunities in their area of interest, providing a streamlined process for both volunteers and organizations.
c

Is ServeSync free to use?


Yes! ServeSync is completely free for students. We are dedicated to providing accessible volunteer opportunities for everyone.
c

How do I sign up for volunteering?


Simply create an account on ServeSync, browse the available opportunities, and sign up for the ones that interest you!
c

Can I track my volunteer hours?


Yes! ServeSync keeps track of your volunteer hours and offers certificates for your contributions.
